Common Queries

Everything you need to know about starting clear aligners at M32 Dental Centre.

Our aligners are manufactured from medical-grade, optically transparent polymers. Unless someone inspects your dental arches from a very close distance, they are practically invisible in daily interactions.
Because they are fabricated digitally and custom-trimmed according to your individual gum line, they do not cause tissue lacerations like metal brackets. You may feel mild pressure during the first 24-48 hours of wearing a new tray set, signifying positive tooth movement.
Most adults and young professionals seeking to correct mild-to-moderate crowding, spacing, overbites, or underbites are excellent candidates. A digital clinical scanning session helps us determine if your structure is ideal for aligner procedures.

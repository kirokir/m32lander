Information Clarification

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear aligners are fabricated from clinical-grade medical polymers with a highly specific light-refractive index. They fit snugly to your teeth, making them nearly invisible at normal conversational distances.

Minor pressure is normal during the first 48 hours of introducing a new alignment set, indicating progressive active movement. Smooth edges are custom trimmed along the gum line to eliminate soft-tissue friction.

Aligner therapy is highly effective for moderate to complex spacing, crowding, and bite variations. A detailed clinical intraoral assessment and 3D dental mapping will determine your exact physiological eligibility.
